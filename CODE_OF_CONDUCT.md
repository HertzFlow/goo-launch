# Code of Conduct

## Pledge

We want this project to be a welcoming place for everyone. All contributors, maintainers, and community participants are expected to behave respectfully.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent.
- Focus on the problem, not personal attributes.
- Give credit where it’s due.

## Unacceptable behavior

We do not tolerate:

- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language.
- Threats or intimidation.
- Publishing private or sensitive information.
- Disruptive or destructive behavior (including repeated spam).

## Reporting

If you witness or experience misconduct, please report it to the maintainers via the repo’s issue tracker or preferred security channel.

## Enforcement

Moderators/maintainers may remove, lock, or close contributions or communications that violate this code of conduct. Repeat offenders may be banned.
